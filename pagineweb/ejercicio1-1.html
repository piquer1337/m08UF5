Death Note

Death Note (stylized as DEATH NOTE) is a Japanese manga series written by Tsugumi Ohba and illustrated by Takeshi Obata.

Plot

In Tokyo, a disaffected high-school student named Light Yagami finds the "Death Note", a mysterious black notebook which can kill anyone as long as the user knows both the targe's name and face.Initally terrified of its god-like power,Light considers the possibilities of the Death Note's abilities and kills high-profile Japanese criminals, then targeting international criminals

Anime

The Death Note anime , directed bt Tetsuro Araki and animated by Madhouse,began airing in Japan on October 3, 2006, and finished its run on June 26, 2007 , totaling 37 twenty-minute episodes.
